# Public Data and Redaction Policy

Work Order: `WO-COUNTY-006`

## Purpose

Provide a conservative review model for County Ops outputs. It does not make a
disclosure decision for a specific record.

The starting authority is the current
[Washington Public Records Act, chapter 42.56 RCW](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=42.56),
together with other applicable law, retention requirements, county policy, and
counsel direction.

## Core Rule

Public availability of one field or record does not automatically authorize
bulk publication, repackaging, model ingestion, external transmission, or
combination with other datasets.

## Classification Before Release

Classify each field and artifact as:

- public and approved for the stated channel;
- public but context-limited or requiring review;
- confidential or exempt;
- security-sensitive;
- operational/internal;
- unknown — hold for records officer or counsel review.

## Minimum Review

1. identify the request, audience, channel, and purpose;
2. identify the official record and system of record;
3. determine applicable disclosure and exemption authority;
4. review owner names, contact details, account identifiers, signatures,
   credentials, case notes, exemption material, images, and free text;
5. assess whether combining fields creates a new privacy or security risk;
6. apply approved redactions to a copy, never the source;
7. preserve a redaction log and cited authority;
8. perform a second-person quality check;
9. release through the approved channel;
10. retain request, response, redaction, and delivery evidence.

## WilliamOS Defaults

- Owner names are excluded from public-oriented outputs by default.
- Credentials, tokens, cookies, connection strings, and internal network
  details are always excluded.
- Free-text notes and attachments require explicit review.
- Screenshots are treated as records containing every visible pixel.
- Synthetic examples must not be reversible to a real person or parcel.
- Unknown classification means hold, not publish.
- No County Ops document may be used as an automatic redaction engine.

## Boundary

This policy is a governance reference, not legal advice and not a records
officer decision. It does not ingest, scan, redact, publish, email, or release
a record.
