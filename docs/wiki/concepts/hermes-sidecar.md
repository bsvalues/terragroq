# Hermes Sidecar

Hermes is the proposed worker sidecar concept.

Hermes may be described as disabled, available, proposed, or authorized depending on future gates.

## Current Posture

Hermes remains blocked. It is not active, not executing, not connected to MCP, and not operating workers.

## Activation Rule

Hermes acts only when authorized by:

1. A scoped Work Order.
2. An authority gate.
3. An activation review.
4. Evidence and safety proof.
5. Primary approval.

## Boundary

This page documents the concept only. It does not activate Hermes.
