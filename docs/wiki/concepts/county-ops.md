## Operating Model

County Ops knowledge may:

- explain generic workflows and evidence contracts;
- link to current public authority;
- define redaction, provenance, review, and stop gates;
- teach Codex and the Primary how to prepare bounded Work Orders.

County Ops knowledge may not:

- connect to PACS or a county network;
- query, export, publish, or write county data;
- contain real parcel, owner, taxpayer, appeal, permit, or exemption records;
- decide a disclosure, valuation, levy, appeal, or legal question;
- activate TerraFusion, Hermes, MCP, a worker, a scheduler, or a command runner.

## Authority

Current law, Department of Revenue guidance, adopted county policy, authorized
officials, records officers, and counsel control live matters. Wiki guidance is
a starting reference and never overrides those authorities.

## Boundary

This concept is static and read-only. Any need for a real system, real record,
specific legal conclusion, or production action is a typed authority wall.
