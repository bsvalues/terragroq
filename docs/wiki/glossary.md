# WilliamOS Glossary

## Primary

The owner and approving authority.

## Operator

The person or bounded agent operating inside authorized scope.

## Codex Operator

The bounded repo operator that executes authorized Work Orders end to end.

## Courier

The limited role of carrying packets and final results between ChatGPT and
Codex when needed. The courier does not perform routine operator work.

## Authority

Explicit permission to perform or prepare a category of work.

## Evidence

Proof of reality: tests, builds, PR checks, production checks, route proof, reports, and safety sweeps.

## Work Order

A bounded unit of governed work.

## Goal

The statement of governed intent and success state.

## Loop

The controlled execution cycle for a batch of Work Orders.

## Brain Council

The advisory reasoning layer. It recommends but does not execute.

## Hermes

A proposed worker sidecar that remains blocked unless separately authorized.

## Agent Forge

The capability preparation area. It does not grant activation by itself.

## Skill

A proposed capability that remains quarantined until evidence and authority
permit activation.

## Quarantine

The safe holding state for unreviewed skills, integrations, or capabilities.

## Memory

Governed continuity context. It does not authorize action.

## Trace Ledger

Static reasoning records and failure-to-eval proposals.

## Eval

A future test or evaluation proposal derived from a classified failure.

## Academy

The governed learning layer.

## Wiki

The governed reference layer.

## County Ops

Static county-operating knowledge placement. It does not touch PACS or county
production systems.

## Production Write

Any action that changes production state, configuration, data, runtime, release,
or external system posture.

## Autonomy

System behavior that acts without an explicit bounded Work Order and owner
authority. It remains blocked unless separately authorized.

## Safety Posture

The explicit record of what remained blocked and unchanged.
